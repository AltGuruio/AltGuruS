About AltGuru

AltGuru is a small team that publishes trade ideas and related tools for people who trade crypto and forex. We are not a broker and not financial advisors; we focus on clear levels, risk notes, and records you can verify.

What we do

AltGuru focuses on traders first: we combine educational context, the technology we use to deliver ideas and records (including Telegram channels and monthly write-ups where we publish them), and practical market experience across cryptocurrency and forex, so members can work setups with clearer structure instead of guessing from headlines alone.

Automation products such as trading bots and the bundler tool are listed separately from subscriptions; scope and billing for each are on the Pricing page.

Markets

We talk about crypto futures, spot, and forex in line with what we actually ship. Some tiers may be marked as not available yet; the site reflects what is open for sale today.

What “signals” means here

A signal is a published trade idea: a suggested place to work a position, a defined point where the idea is wrong, and levels for taking profit or managing the trade. It is not an instruction to trade, and it is not personalized advice for your account size or jurisdiction.

You choose whether to follow an idea, how much to risk, and which venue you use. Past signals and reports do not predict future results.

How we think about risk

Leverage and volatility can wipe out accounts quickly. We try to keep risk language next to opportunity so it is harder to skim past. We still expect every member to read disclaimers on this site and only trade money they can afford to lose.

We try to

  • Log what we publish so it can be reviewed later
  • Keep formats readable on a phone
  • Point to results and pricing instead of vague promises

We do not

  • Manage your account or place trades for you
  • Replace your own research or compliance obligations
